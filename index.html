Our Goals

What We Set Out to Achieve

Project CRESS and Project Sinaran are anchored by clear national, environmental, and economic objectives that benefit Malaysia and the region.

🌞

100% Green Energy Supply

Produce and deliver fully renewable electricity to corporate offtakers 24 hours a day through solar generation and BESS-backed nighttime supply.

📋

ESG Compliance Enablement

Help Bursa-listed companies and MNCs meet mandatory ESG reporting, reduce carbon tax exposure, and qualify for Green SRI Sukuk financing incentives.

🇲🇾

National Energy Transformation

Advance Malaysia's NETR roadmap — targeting 70% renewable energy by 2050 — through one of the largest private-funded RE projects in the country.

💼

Foreign Direct Investment

Bring RM20–27 billion in FDI from global Fortune 500 partners with zero government funding, supporting Malaysia's DDI and FDI KPIs.

👷

Jobs & Local Development

Create 20,000–30,000 jobs with 75% local workforce, TVET and university sponsorship, and awarding 70% of works to local contractors and SMEs.

🌏

ASEAN Regional Leadership

Position Malaysia as a major green energy exporter to Singapore, underpin the ASEAN Power Grid, and materialize the Malaysia-Singapore Energy Exchange.

What We Offer

Our Services

From green energy supply to ESG advisory, CRESS delivers end-to-end renewable solutions for medium and high voltage corporate customers.

⚡

24-Hour Green Energy Supply

Uninterrupted 100% renewable electricity via solar during daylight and BESS storage after sunset — available to medium and high voltage customers with minimum 10,000 MWh/year offtake.

📜

M-REC Certification

Malaysian Renewable Energy Certificates (M-REC) provided free of charge with every energy supply contract, enabling companies to demonstrate verified green energy consumption for ESG reporting.

📊

ESG Compliance Support

Support your company's ESG journey — from reducing Scope 2 emissions and carbon tax exposure, to qualifying for green financing incentives like Green SRI Sukuk at lower interest rates.

🔋

Battery Energy Storage (BESS)

10,000 MWh integrated BESS ensures stable, dispatchable energy delivery around the clock — making renewable energy as reliable as conventional power for critical operations.

🌾

Agrivoltaic Development

Optional agrivoltaic integration combines solar power generation with agricultural activities, supporting food security and maximising land use across the 3,000-acre development phases.

🚀

Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

25-year fixed-rate bilateral energy supply contract under the ST CRESS framework, providing long-term price certainty and regulatory security compared to monthly TNB tariff adjustments.
